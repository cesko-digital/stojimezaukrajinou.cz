The courage to help others has won!

We chose the right moment to band together and step out of the crowd to lend a helping hand to those who were in need of it.

Together and systematically #StandWithUkraine.

24 February 2022. The war in Ukraine broke out. We were shaken and wanted to help.

Speed was key!

Our solution was finalized within 3 days. The idea of a help directory sprung up in multiple places at once. Česko.Digital took it under its wing in the very beginnings and supported it with the force of their active community of expert volunteers. Thanks to us making use of our three-year experience and the technical support of solidpixels., the directory was completed pretty much within one weekend. A number of volunteers, organizations and companies then helped to further improve and promote it. By working together, we have created the informational directory Stojíme za Ukrajinou.

Source: solidpixels.

🗓 24 February 2022

24.2.2022 is not just the day when the war in Ukraine started, it is also the day when a huge wave of solidarity arose.

24.2.2022 is the day when we could all witness that there are many true heroes among us!

For maximum clarity and to lessen information fragmentation.

We created a website which simplified the process for those who wanted to help but did not know how (I want to help), and to those who needed the help but did not know where to turn (I need help). So, the directory was created in 4 language variations.

The numbers were incredible!

In the first week since the launch the directory was visited by 156 000 people.

And after one month, the site was visited by 353 000 users.

Why was it really worth the effort?

Throughout its full run, the website was visited by 771 000 people.
The directory was helping not only those coming into the Czech Republic, but was visited by a further 83 200 people from within the Ukrainian territory looking for help.

4 language variations

🇨🇿 & 🇬🇧 "How to help" and 🇺🇦 & 🇷🇺 "I am seeking help".
370 000 people sought help on the website.
217 000 people offered help.

An organic content growth. The public gets involved.

After the directory’s creation, on 27.2. a pool of suggestions from the public was opened, with the intent to continue improving the directory’s content. In total, 255 ideas from users were collected.
142 entries were added based on information gathered from the public.

13 categories for aid were created.

What do the people involved say?

Katka

“The war in Ukraine caused a society-wide shock. The ambition of the directory was to contribute to the managing of this emergency situation - quickly and effectively. Its success was the work of many people. Those who helped to create the directory, to perfect and promote it. And also those who hold the banner of aid long-term, in nonprofit organizations, companies, institutions, or as individual people. They help to fortify the topic of human rights, to solve challenges in society, or to care for communities. Thanks to them the directory got the opportunity to exist. The role of Česko.Digital was irreplacable. Its patronage over the project when it was most needed brought with it the credibility of a well-known brand, and a space of a diverse, active and strong community of expert volunteers.” - Kateřina Jiřinová, Product Owner

Jiří

„Every time something like this happens, there are many organizations and people who want to help. They wait around for a bit  to see if anything will happen, and if nothing does, they start its realization themselves. That’s how people end up working on the same things at the same time. The Česko.Digital community’s contribution provided the experts from various fields a neutral ground from which they could join forces and help effectively.“ - Jiří Sekera, volunteer and one of the main coordinators of the project

Adina

„To see the huge wave of solidarity and to help it aim for the right places through the rapidly emerging project is like a dream. The team on the side of Česko.Digital and the volunteers were both a delight to work with. Thank you for the opportunity to be there.“ - Adina Foxová, designer in the Stojíme za Ukrajinou project

Tereza

„We once again managed to connect worlds which speak different languages but together can accomplish incredible things – tech firms and the nonprofit sector. Our main goal was to immediately create centralized spaces for help to everyone fleeing the war into our country. Within one day the stojimezaukrajinou.cz website was visited by around 100 thousand people. They were either entering or wanted to aid Ukraine and wanted to get familiar with a new environment in their own language.“ - Tereza Gagnon, Head of Partnership & Fundraising Česko.Digital

Eva

„Synergy and cooperation are key components for long-term and lasting aid at this time. The expert volunteer union in our Česko.Digital community once again showed that they can join forces within an extremely short time frame and use their expertise for essential projects impacting the whole society. It is important to say that we could not have accomplished such a result were it not for the long-term support of expert volunteering within the community. Great admiration is due for all who were involved.“ - Eva Pavlíková, CEO of the community of expert volunteers

No fragmentation of efforts, but joining forces. There is power in community.
